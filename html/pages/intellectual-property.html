4Knines, LLC (“4Knines”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is in the business of developing innovative products for canines and their owners under the 4Knines® brand. 4Knines is a pioneer in the field of vehicle seat covers. For example, 4Knines was the first to design, manufacture, and sell vehicle covers that are adaptable to multiple vehicle seat configurations, namely via sections that can be used with various 60-40 or 40-20-40 split bench seats. By virtue of its patent portfolio, 4Knines is the only legally authorized provider of such adaptable vehicle seat covers in the United States.
