Return Policy

What is your return policy?

4Knines products are backed by a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. We want you to be happy! You may return your 4Knines cover for any reason within the first 30 days for a full refund (minus return shipping charges).

How do I submit a return?

Please email your order information to support@4Knines.com for assistance with your return within 30-days of purchase. We will provide a Return Authorization Number and return instructions. If you need any assistance call 888-602-2509.

How soon after I return the item will I see my refund?

Once you return your 4Knines cover, send tracking number to support@4Knines.com. We will issue a refund when the cover is received in our warehouse. You will receive an email confirming your refund status, and the refund will appear in your account within a few days.

Shipping Information

Do you offer free shipping?

We offer free standard shipping (5-7 business days) to addresses in the continental US. Shipping to non-continental US is $9.99. Expedited shipping (2-3 business days) is available within the continental US for a flat fee of $7.99.

Where do you ship?

We ship to all US addresses. Unfortunately, international shipping is not available.

When will my package arrive?

Orders with standard shipping typically arrive within 7 business days. Expedited orders arrive in 2-3 business days. Orders shipped to Alaska and Hawaii take a bit longer. You will receive tracking details when your order ships.
