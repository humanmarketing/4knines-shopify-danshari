When you purchase a 4Knines cover, you are getting the best dog cover on the market AND you are supporting the pet community. At 4Knines, we believe in giving back! Since day one, 4Knines has donated a percentage of every sale to animal advocacy groups. In addition, we support and showcase the great work of various animal advocacy groups in our Monthly Spotlight articles. You can find out more about our giving back campaigns on the 4Knines Blog.

Pawsitive Love Foundation

Pawsitive Love Foundation provides trained service dogs and handler training to help bring bring a measure of freedom through healing, helping, and support to veterans and children. Your gift will help us meet our goals of helping children and veterans in need of a properly trained service dog.

National Disaster Search Dog Foundation

National Disaster Search Dog Foundation offers professionally trained canines and ongoing training programs at no cost to first responders to make sure that when disaster strikes, no one is left behind. They also ensure lifetime care for every dog in their program: once rescued, these dogs never need to be rescued again. Your support will make a difference and help save the lives of dogs and humans alike.

Healing4Heroes

Healing4Heroes helps our nation’s heroes overcome the visible and invisible wounds of war while also training abandoned and neglected dogs as ADA compliant service dogs. Serving a dual-purpose, your donation helps save two lives at the same time: a VET and a PET!

Project Freedom Ride

Project Freedom Ride rescues unwanted dogs from high-kill shelters in Texas and connects them with loving families in the Pacific Northwest. Seven-year-old Roman is the mastermind and spokesperson behind Project Freedom Rode, which has facilitated the rescue of more than 1,000 dogs from euthanasia. Project Freedom Ride is 100% donation-driven and needs your help to continue expanding their safe havens.

America’s VetDogs

America's VetDogs helps provide renewed independence and enhanced mobility to veterans, active-duty service members, and first responders with disabilities. Your donation will help place specially trained guide and service dogs free of charge to veterans and first responders who have served our country honorably.

Dogs on Deployment

Dogs on Deployment provides military pet owners peace of mind as they serve our country with honor, courage, and commitment. No pet should be surrendered to a shelter because of a military commitment. Your donations help Dogs on Deployment find volunteer boarders willing to care for military members pets during times of need.

D.E.L.T.A. Rescue

D.E.L.T.A. Rescue (Dedication and Everlasting Love to Animals) is the largest “No Kill, Care-for-Life” sanctuary of its kind in the world. All D.E.L.T.A animals receive lots of love and any necessary veterinary care while living the rest of their lives at the beautifully landscaped mountaintop ranch, never having to worry about being lost and alone again. Your life-saving gifts will provide sanctuary for rescued animals who were abandoned or born in the wilderness.

Paws for Purple Hearts (PPH)

Paws for Purple Hearts (PPH) serves veterans facing mobility challenges and trauma-related conditions by providing them with high-quality service dogs. Through Canine-Assisted Warrior Therapy, veterans with PTSD are taught to train service dogs for comrades with physical disabilities and combat-related injuries. Thanks to your generous donations, hearts and minds are healed, and warriors lives are changed.

Warrior Canine Connection

The Warrior Canine Connection involves veterans in every step of the process by utilizing a Mission-Based Trauma Recovery model. With this approach, training one dog can help as many as 60 warriors recover from the stress of combat. Your tax-deductible donation helps Warrior Canine Connection provide highly-skilled assistance dogs to wounded veterans at absolutely no charge.

Refurbished Pets of Southern Michigan (RPSM)

Refurbished Pets of Southern Michigan (RPSM) helps prepare dogs for new homes while also enriching the lives of the inmates who care for them. This highly successful program serves as a form of rehabilitation for inmates and dogs alike...allowing both to reenter society as valued and productive members. Your help is needed to give deserving dogs a second chance through the Canine Companion Program.

Old Dog Haven

Old Dog Haven utilizes a large network of volunteers to provide secure homes for senior dogs who have been abandoned. Dogs 8 years and older are rescued from shelters and placed in temporary or permanent homes. When too frail for adoption, loving families provide comfort and quality of life during the senior dog's final days. Your gift helps Old Dog Haven continue to help senior dogs in need.

Foothills Animal Rescue

Located in Scottsdale, AZ, Foothills Animal Rescue is a cage-free, adoption-guaranteed nonprofit dedicated to saving lives through the rescue, care, and adoption of homeless cats and dogs. At Foothills Animal Rescue’s state-of-the-art facility, dogs are free to romp in indoor/outdoor runs, and cats nestle on comfortable perches while awaiting their forever homes.
